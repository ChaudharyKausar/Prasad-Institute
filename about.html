About Prasad Institute

Prasad Institute is a leading coaching institute dedicated to preparing students for NEET, JEE, and MHT-CET. With a science-focused approach, we provide deep conceptual clarity, strategic test planning, and one-on-one mentorship for 11th & 12th students.

Our mission is to make top-quality education accessible and stress-free — with Pay After Result options and scholarships of up to ₹4 lakh for deserving students.

Our success isn’t limited to India— Prasad students have also made it to top medical and engineering colleges in the USA, proving our global academic standard.

Founder & Director – Prasad Institute

Meet the Founder

Mr. Prasad Dattatray Sawant began teaching in 2009, while pursuing his B.Sc-IT. His passion for education grew stronger, leading him to teach higher classes and work full-time by 2014. On 31st May 2015, he founded Prasad Institute to deliver focused, quality-driven education. He holds degrees in B.Sc-IT, B.A., M.A. (Education) and is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Education. With over a decade of teaching experience, he believes in guiding each student with personal attention and academic excellence. education.
